Verse 171Verse 7.5lus kyi dge ba rnams bya źiṅ ||

Verse 176Verse 7.10brtan pa rnams kyis lus bsdam źiṅ ||

Verse 178Verse 7.12ṅag rnams bsruṅ źiṅ yid ni legs bsdams te ||
