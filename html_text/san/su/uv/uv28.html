Udānavarga

Pāpavarga

sarvapāpasyākaraṇaṃ
kuśalasyopasaṃpadaḥ |
svacittaparyavadanam
etad buddhasya śāsanam ||

dadataḥ puṇyaṃ pravardhate
vairaṃ na kriyato ca saṃyamāt |
kuśalī prajahāti pāpakaṃ
rāgadoṣamohakṣayāt tu nirvṛtiḥ ||

sārdhaṃ carann ckakaḥ sadā
miśro hy anyajanena vedakaḥ |
kuśalī prajahāti pāpakaṃ
krauñcaḥ kṣīrapako yathodakam ||

dṛṣṭvā hy ādīnavaṃ loke
jñātvā dharmaniraupadhim |
āryo na ramate pāpe
pāpo na ramate śubhe ||

praviv­eka­rasaṃ jñatvā
rasaṃ copaśamasya vai
nirjvaro bhavati niṣpāpo
dharmaprītirasaṃ piban ||

anavasruta­cittas­ya
tv anunnahanacctasaḥ |
puṇyapāpaprahīṇasya
nāsti durgatito bhayam ||

niṣed­dhāraṃ pravaktāraṃ
yaj jāned vadyadarśinam |
nigṛhyavādinaṃ dhīraṃ
tādrśaṃ satataṃ bhajet |
tādrśaṃ bhajamānasya
śreyo bhavati na pāpakam ||

upaśānto hy uparato
mandabhāsī hy anuddhataḥ |
dhunāti pāpakāṃ dharmāṃ
drurnapatraṃ yathānilaḥ ||

yo hy apraduṣṭasya narasya duṣyate
śuddhasya nityaṃ vigatāṅgaṇasya
tam eva bālaṃ pratiyāti pāpaṃ
kṣiptaṃ rajaḥ prativātaṃ yathaiva ||

yad yat karoti puruṣas
tat tat paśyati hātmanaḥ |
kalyāṇakārī kalyāṇaṃ
pāpakārī ca pāpakam ||

ātmana hi kṛte pāpe
tv ātmanā kliśyate sadā |
ātmanā tv akṛte pāpe
hy ātmanaiva viśudhyate ||

aśuddhabuddhiṃ pratyātmaṃ
nānyo hy anyaṃ viśodhayet |
abhimathnāti taṃ pāpaṃ
vajram aśmamaṇiṃ yathā ||

cakṣuṣmāṃ viṣamānīva
vidyamāne parākramet |
paṇḍito jīvaloko ’smiṃ
pāpāni parivarjayet ||

vaṇig vā sabhayaṃ mārgam
alpaśāstro mahādhano |
viṣaṃ jīvitakāmo va
pāpāni parivarjayet ||

pāṇau cāsya vraṇo na syād
dhārayet pāṇinā viṣam |
nāvraṇe krāmati viṣaṃ
nāsti pāpam akurvataḥ ||

sukarāṇi hy asādhūni
svātmano hy ahitāni ca |
yad vai hitaṃ ca pathyaṃ ca
tad vai paramaduṣkaram ||

sukaraṃ sādhunā sadhu
sādhu pāpena duṣkaram |
pāpaṃ pāpena sukaraṃ
pāpam āryeṇa duṣkaram ||

madhuvad manyate bālo
yāvat pāpaṃ na pacyate |
yadā tu pacyate pāpam
atha duḥkhaṃ nigacchati ||

pāpo ’pi paśyate bhadraṃ
yāvat pāpaṃ na pacyate |
yadā tu pacyate pāpam
atha pāpāni paśyati ||

bhadro ’pi paśyate pāpaṃ
yāvad bhadraṃ na pacyate |
yadā tu pacyate bhadram
atha bhadrāṇi paśyati ||

kuryāc cet puruṣaḥ pāpaṃ
nainaṃ kuryāt punaḥ punaḥ |
na tatra cchandaṃ kurvīta
duḥkhaṃ pāpaśya saṃcayaḥ ||

kuryāt tu puruṣaḥ puṇyaṃ
kuryāc cainaṃ punaḥ punaḥ |
tatra cchandaṃ ca kurvīta
sukhaṃ puṇyasya saṃcayaḥ ||

abhitvareta kalyāṇe
pāpāc cittaṃ nivārayet |
dhandhaṃ hi kurvataḥ puṇyaṃ
pāpesu ramate manaḥ ||

alpakaṃ pi kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ
duḥkhāya parataḥ sadā |
mahate bhavaty anarthāya
visaṃ koṣṭhagataṃ yathā ||

alpakaṃ pi kṛtaṃ puṇyaṃ
paraloke sukhāvaham |
arthāya mahate nityaṃ
sasyānām iva saṃcayaḥ ||

adaṇḍeṣu hi daṇḍena
yo ’praduṣṭesu duṣyate |
daśānām anyatamaṃ sthānaṃ
kṣipram eva nigacchati ||

jñātīnām vā vinābhāvaṃ
bhogānāṃ vā parikṣayam |
rājato hy upasargaṃ vāpy
abhyākhyānaṃ ca dāruṇam ||

vedanāṃ kaṭukāṃ vāpi
śarīrasya ca bhedanam |
ābādhaṃ vāpi paruṣaṃ
cittakṣepam athāpi vā ||

atha vāsyāpy agārāṇi
hy agnir dahati sarvathā |
bhedāt kāyasya cāprājño
daśamāṃ durgatiṃ vrajet ||

pāpe tu kṛte hi nāśvasec
cirakṛte dūrakṛte ’pi nāśvaset |
rahasi ca kṛte ’pi nāśvased
asti tasya vipāka iti nāśvaset ||

puṇye tu kṛte tv ihāśvacec
cirakṛte dūrakṛte ’pi cāsvaset |
rahasi ca kṛte ’pi cāśvased
asti tasya vipāka iti cāśvaset ||

pāpe tu kṛte hi śocate
cirakṛte dūrakṛte ’pi śocate |
rahasi ca kṛte ’pi śocate
asti tasya vipāka iti śocate ||

puṇye tu kṛte hi nandate
cirakṛte dūrakṛte ’pi nandate |
rahasi ca kṛte ’pi nandate
asti tasya vipāka iti nandate ||

iha śocati pretya śocati
pāpakarmā hy ubhayatra śocati
sa hi śocati sa praśocati
dṛṣṭvā karma hi kliṣṭam ātmanaḥ ||

iha nandati pretya nandati
kṛtapuṇyo hy ubhayatra nandati |
sa hi nandati sa pramodate
dṛṣṭvā karma viśuddham ātmanaḥ ||

pāpe tu kṛte hi śocate
cirakṛte dūrakṛte ’pi socate |
rahasi ca kṛte ’pi śocate
bhūyaḥ śocati durgatiṃ gataḥ ||

puṇye tu kṛte hi nandate
cirakṛte dūrakṛte ’pi nandate |
rahasi ca kṛte ’pi nandate
bhūyo nandati sadgatiṃ gataḥ ||

puṇye tu kṛte hi modate
cirakṛte dūrakṛte ’pi modate |
rahasi ca kṛte ’pi modate
bhūyo modati sadgatiṃ gataḥ ||

kṛte ca pāpe ’py akṛte ca puṇye
’dharmaṃ samādāya vihāya dharmam |
bibheti mṛtyor iha pāpakarmā
bhinnaplavo madhya ivodakasya ||

kṛtaṃ ca puṇyaṃ hy akṛtaṃ ca pāpam
satāṃ ca dharmaś caritaḥ purāṇaḥ |
bibheti mṛtyor na kadaṃ cid eva
yathaiva nāvā dṛdhayā tarantaḥ ||

|| pāpavargaḥ || ||
