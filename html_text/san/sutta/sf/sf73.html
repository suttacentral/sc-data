Pravāraṇasūtra

sugata pratibhātu te vāgīśa bhagavān avocat athāyuṣmāṃ vāgīśas tasyāṃ velāyāṃ gāthāṃ babhāṣe ||

Hoernle 1iha paṃcaHoernle 1iha paṃcadaśī viśuddhikā samitā |
paṃcaśatāś ca bhikṣavaḥ saṃyojana bandhana |
cchidaḥ sarve kṣīṇabhavā maharṣayaḥ ||

Hoernle 2śuddhā upāsate śuddhaṃ vipramuktapunarbhaHoernle 2śuddhā upāsate śuddhaṃ vipramuktapunarbhavā |
prahīṇa-jāti-maranāḥ kṛtakṛtyā nirāsravāḥ ||

Hoernle 3audhatyavicikitsecchā mānaHoernle 3audhatyavicikitsecchā mānagranthabhavacchidaḥ
tṛṣṇā-śalyasya hartāro ’cita-tṛṣṇā-punarbhavāḥ ||

Hoernle 4siṃho ’si nirupādānaṃ prahīṇa-bhaya-bhairava |
upadhiṃ samatikrāntaḥ āsravā nihatas tvayā ||

Hoernle 5cakravarti yathā rājā sacivaiḥ parivāritaḥ |
samaṃtād anuśāstīmāṃ sāgarāṃtāṃ vasundharām ||

Hoernle 6tathā vijīta saṃgrāmaṃ sārthavāham anuttaram |
upāsate śravakāstvā traividyā-mṛtyu-hāyina ||

Hoernle 7putrās te sarva evaite plāvī hy atra na vidyate |
hartāraṃ sarva śalyānāṃ vande tvāditya-bāndhavam ||

idam avocad bhagavān āptamanasas te bhikṣavo bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandaṃ ||

pravāraṇa sūtraṃ ||

Hoernle, A. F. Rudolf 1916.
