Veṇukātyāyanasagotrīsūtra

Wal 1 pṛccha bhagini śrutvā te vedayiṣyāmi |

Wal 2 saṃti śramaṇa eke śramaṇa brāhmaṇāḥ ye svayaṃkṛtaṃ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñapayaṃti | saṃty eke parakṛtaṃ prajñapayaṃti saṃty eke svayaṃkṛtaṃ ca parakṛtaṃ ca | saṃty eke ’svayaṃkāra?aparakārahetusamutpannaṃ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñapayaṃti | yathā kathaṃ samutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñapayaṃti |

Wal 3 anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñapayaṃti |

Wal 4 yathā kathaṃ śramaṇa anyathāsamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñapayaṃti |

Wal 5 pratītyasamutpannaṃ bhagini arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñapayaṃti |

Wal 6 yathā kathaṃ śramaṇa pratītyasamutpannaṃ arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñpayaṃti |

Wal 7 tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaitaṃ vyākuru |

Wal 8 kiṃ manyase bhagini | asti cakṣuḥ | asti śramaṇa | asti rūpam | asti śramaṇa |

Wal 9 cakṣuś ced bhagini utpadyeta api nu asti cakṣuḥsaṃsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā duḥkhaṃ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṃ vā | asti śramaṇa |

Wal 10 asti śrotram asti ghāṇam asti jihvā asti kāyaḥ asti manaḥ |

asti śramaṇa | saṃti dharmāḥ |

Wal 11 asti śrotraṃ ghrāṇaṃ jihvā kāyo manaś ced utpadyeta api nu śrotraghrāṇajihvākāyamanaḥsaṃsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā duḥkhaṃ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṃ vā |

asti śramaṇa |

Wal 12 evaṃ hi bhagini pratīyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhaṃ prajñapayaṃti |

Wal 13 evaṃ hi śramaṇa pratītyasamutpannam arhantaḥ sukhaṃ prajñapya yathā kathaṃ śramaṇa arhantaḥ sukhaduḥkhasya nirodhaṃ prajñapayaṃti |

Wal 14 tena hi bhagini tvām eva prakṣyāmi yathā te kṣamati tathaitaṃ vyākuru |

Wal 15 kiṃ manyase bhagini | cakṣuś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarvaṃ sarvathā apariśeṣaṃ nirudhyeta apariśeṣan adhyastaṃ parikṣayam paryādānaṃ gacched api nu cakṣuḥsaṃsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā duḥkhaṃ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṃ vā |

no śramaṇa |

Wal 16 śrotraṃ ghrāṇaṃ jihvā kāyo manaś ced bhagini sarveṇa sarvaṃ sarvathā apariśeṣaṃ nirudhyeta apariśeṣam adhyastaṃ parikṣayaṃ paryādānaṃ gacchet api nu manaḥsaṃsparśapratyayam ādhyātmam utpadyate vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā duḥkhaṃ vā aduḥkhāsukhaṃ vā |

no śramaṇa |

Wal 17 evaṃ hi bhagini ārhantaḥ pratītyasamutpannasya sukhaduḥkhasya nirodhaṃ prajñapayaṃti |

Wal 18 asmiṃ khalu dharmaparyāye bhāṣyamāne veṇukātyāyanasagotryā virajo vigatamalaṃ dharmeṣu dharmacakṣur utpannam …

Wal 19 atha veṇukātyāyanasagotrī dṛṣṭadharmā prāptadharmā viditadharmā paryavagāḍhadharmā tīrṇakāṃkṣā tīrṇavicikitsā aparapratyayā ananyaneyā śāstuḥ śāsane dharmeṣu vaiśaradyaprāptā utthāyāsanād ekāṃsam uttarāsaṃgaṃ kṛtvā yenāyuṣmān udāyī tenāṃjaliṃ praṇamya āyuṣmantam udāyinam idam avocat |

Wal 20 abhikrāntāhaṃ śramaṇa abhikrāntā eṣāhaṃ bhagavantaṃ śaraṇaṃ gacchāmi dharmaṃ ca bhikṣusaṃghaṃ ca | upāsikāṃ ca mān dhāraya śramaṇa adyāgreṇa yāvajjīvaṃ prāṇopetaṃ śaraṇaṃgatām abhiprasannām |

Wal 21 atha udāyī veṇukātyāyanasagotrīṃ brāhmaṇīṃ dhārmyā kathayāsaṃdarśayitvā samādapayitvā samuttejayitvā saṃpraharṣayitvā utthāyāsanāt prakrāntaḥ |

Waldschmidt, Ernst 1980c. On a Sanskrit version of the Verahaccāni Sutta of the Saṃyuttanikāya. NAWG 1980/4: 69–76. Reprinted in AKS, 362–369.
