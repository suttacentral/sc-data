16. śukapotakāvadānam

Vai 123.001 śrāvastyāṃ nidānam |
Vai 123.002 tena khalu samayena anāthapiṇḍadena gṛhaptinā dvau śukaśāvakau pratilabdhau |
Vai 123.003 tena niveśanaṃ nītvā ālāpitau poṣitau saṃvardhitau mānuṣālāpaṃ ca śikṣāpitau |
Vai 123.004 tayoścāyuṣmānānando ’bhīkṣṇamāgatya caturāryasatyasamprativedhikīṃ dharmadeśanāṃ karoti—yaduta idaṃ duḥkham, ayaṃ duḥkhasamudayaḥ, ayaṃ duḥkhanirodhaḥ, iyaṃ duḥkhanirodhagāminī pratipaditi |
Vai 123.005 sthavirasthavirā api bhikṣavo ’nāthapiṇḍadasya gṛhapater niveśanamupasaṃkrāmanti śāriputramaudgalyāyanakāśyapānandaraivataprabhṛtayaḥ |
Vai 123.007 teṣāṃ kālānukālamupasaṃkrāmatāṃ tābhyāṃ śukaśāvakābhyāṃ nāmāni parijñātāni |
Vai 123.008 yāvadapareṇa samayenāyuṣmāñ śāriputro ’nāthapiṇḍadasya gṛhapater niveśanamanuprāptaḥ |
Vai 123.011 adrāṣṭāṃ tau śukaśāvakau āyuṣmantaṃ śāriputram |
Vai 123.009 dṛṣṭvā antarjanamāmantrayatah—eṣa bhadantaḥ sthaviraḥ śāriputra āgacchati, āsanamasya prajñāpayateti |
Vai 123.010 evamāyuṣmantaṃ mahāmaudgalyāyanaṃ kāśyapaṃ raivatamāyuṣmantamānandaṃ dṛṣṭvā kathayatah—eṣo ’smākamācāryānanda āgacchati, āsanamasya prajñāpayateti |
Vai 123.012 yāvadapareṇa samayena bhagavānanāthapiṇḍadasya gṛhapater niveśanamanuprāptaḥ |
adrāṣṭāṃ tau śukaśāvakau bhagavantaṃ dūrādevāgacchantaṃ prāsādikaṃ prasādanīyaṃ śāntendriyaṃ śāntamānasaṃ parameṇa cittamatyupaśamena samanvāgataṃ suvarṇayūpamiva śriyā jvalantam |
Vai 123.014 dṛṣṭvā ca punastvaritatvaritamantarjanamāmantrayatah—eṣa bhadanto bhagavānāgacchati, āsanamasya prajñāpayateti hṛṣṭamadhurasvareṇa nikūjataḥ |
Vai 123.016 atha bhagavāṃstayoranugrahārthaṃ praviśya prajñapta evāsane niṣaṇṇaḥ |
niṣadya bhagavatā śukaśāvakau caturāryasatyasamprativedhikayā dharmadeśanayā śaraṇagamanaśikṣāpadeṣu pratiṣṭhāpitau |
Vai 123.018 atha bhagavāñ śukaśāvakau antarjanaṃ ca dharmyayā kathayā saṃdarśya samādāpya samuttejya saṃpraharṣyotthāyāsanāt prakrāntaḥ |
Vai 123.019 tau cāntarjanasya viharataḥ pramādavihāriṇau biḍālena prāṇinā gṛhītau |
Vai 123.020 vihvalavadanau cchidyamāneṣu marmasu mucyamāneṣu saṃdhiṣu namo buddhāya, namo dharmāya, namaḥ saṃghāyetyuktvā kālagatau cāturmahārājakāyikeṣu deveṣūpapannau |
|
Vai 123.022 atha bhagavānanyatamasmin pradeśe smitamakārṣīt |
adrākṣīdāyuṣmānānando bhagavantaṃ smitaṃ prāviṣkurvantam |
Vai 123.023 dṛṣṭvā ca punarbhagavantamidamavocat—nāhetupratyayaṃ bhadanta tathāgatā arhantaḥ samyaksambuddhāḥ smitaṃ prāviṣkurvanti |
Vai 123.024 ko bhadanta hetuḥ kaḥ pratyayaḥ smitasya prāviṣkaraṇe? evametadānanda, evametat |
Vai 123.025 nāhetupratyayaṃ tathāgatā arhantaḥ samyaksambuddhāḥ smitaṃ prāviṣkurvanti |
Vai 123.026 dṛṣṭau tvayā ānanda tau śukaśāvakau? dṛṣṭau bhadanta |
tau ānanda śukaśāvakau mama samanantaraprakrāntasya biḍālena prāṇinā jīvitād vyaparopitau |
Vai 123.027 tau buddhadharmasaṃghāvalambanayā smṛtyā kālagatau cāturmahārājakāyikeṣu deveṣūpapannau |
|
Vai 123.029 atha saṃbahulā bhikṣavaḥ pūrvāhṇe nivāsya pātracīvaramādāya śrāvastīṃ piṇḍāya prāvikṣan |
Vai 123.030 aśrauṣuḥ saṃbahulā bhikṣavaḥ śrāvastīṃ piṇḍāya pracaranto ’nāthapiṇḍadasya gṛhapater niveśane śukaśāvakau—namo buddhāya, namo dharmāya, namaḥ saṃghāyeti biḍālena prāṇinā vyaparopitauVai 124.000 iti | Vai 123.030 aśrauṣuḥ saṃbahulā bhikṣavaḥ śrāvastīṃ piṇḍāya pracaranto ’nāthapiṇḍadasya gṛhapater niveśane śukaśāvakau—namo buddhāya, namo dharmāya, namaḥ saṃghāyeti biḍālena prāṇinā vyaparopitauVai 124.000 iti | Vai 124.001

śrutvā ca punaḥ śrāvastīṃ piṇḍāya caritvā kṛtabhaktakṛtyāḥ paścādbhaktapiṇḍapātapratikrāntāḥ pātracīvaraṃ pratisāmayya pādau prakṣālya yena bhagavāṃstenopasaṃkrāntāḥ |
Vai 124.002 upasaṃkramya bhagavataḥ pādau śirasā vanditvā ekānte niṣaṇṇāḥ |
Vai 124.003 ekāntaniṣaṇṇāḥ saṃbahulā bhikṣavo bhagavantamidamavocan—iha vayaṃ bhadanta saṃbahulā bhikṣavaḥ pūrvavad yāvadanāthapiṇḍadasya gṛhapater niveśane dvau śukaśāvakau—namo buddhāya, namo dharmāya, namaḥ saṃghāyeti kurvāṇau biḍālena prāṇinā jīvitādvyaparopitau iti |
Vai 124.006 tayorbhadanta kā gatiḥ, kopapattiḥ, ko ’bhisamparāyah? bhagavānāha—tau bhikṣavaḥ śukaśāvakau tasya śaraṇagamanasya vipākena ṣaṭtriṃśatkṛtvaścāturmahārājakāyikeṣu deveṣūpapatsyete, ṣaṭtriṃśatkṛtvastrāyastriṃśeṣu, yāmeṣu, tuṣiteṣu, nirmāṇaratiṣu, paranirmitavaśavartiṣu deveṣūpapastyete |
124.009 tatastāvat ṣaṭsu kāmāvacareṣu deveṣu sattvā vyapasaṃsṛtya paścime bhave paścime nikete paścime ātmabhāvapratilambhe manuṣyapratilābhaṃ labdhvā pratyekāṃ bodhimabhisambhotsyete, dharmaśca sudharmaśca pratyekabuddhau bhaviṣyataḥ |
Vai 124.011 evaṃ hi bhikṣavo mahāphalaṃ dharmaśravaṇaṃ mahānuśaṃsakam, kaḥ punarvādo dharmadeśanā dharmābhisamayo vā |
Vai 124.012 tasmāttarhi bhikṣava evaṃ śikṣitavyam yanno dharmaśravaṇābhiratā bhaviṣyāmaḥ |
Vai 124.013 ityevaṃ vo bhikṣavaḥ śikṣitavyam |
|
Vai 124.014 idamavocadbhagavān |
ātamanasaste bhikṣavo bhagavato bhāṣitamabhyanandan |
|
Vai 124.015 iti śrīdivyāvadāne śukapotakāvadānaṃ ṣoḍaśam |
|
