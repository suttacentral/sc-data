25. sa gharakṣitāvadānam (2)

Vai 215.001bhikṣavaḥ saṃśayajātāḥ sarvasaṃśayacchettāraṃ buddhaṃ bhagavantaṃ pṛcchanti—kiṃ bhadanta āyuṣmatā saṃgharakṣitena karma kṛtam, yasya karmaṇo vipākenāḍhye mahābhoge kule jāto bhagavato ’ntike pravrajya sarvakleśaprahāṇādarhattvaṃ sākṣātkṛtam? evaṃ taṃ ca vaineyakāryaṃ kṛtam? bhagavānāha—saṃgharakṣitenaiva bhikṣavaḥ karmāni kṛtāni upacitāni pūrvavat |
Vai 215.005 bhūtapūrvaṃ bhikṣavo ’sminn eva bhadrake kalpe viṃśativarṣasahasrāyuṣi prajāyāṃ nāma śāstā pūrvavat |
Vai 215.006 tasyāyaṃ śāsane pravrajita āsīdvaiyāvṛtyakaraḥ |
Vai 215.007 asya tatra pañca sārdhaṃvihāriśatāni |
yadbhūyasā ekakarvaṭanivāsī janakāyaḥ, asyaivābhiprasannaḥ |
Vai 215.008 anena tatra yāvadāyuḥparyantaṃ brahmacaryaṃ cīrṇam, na kaścidguṇo ’dhigataḥ |
Vai 215.009 apareṇa samayena glānībhūtaḥ |
mūlagaṇḍapatrapuṣpaphalabhaiṣajyairupasthīyamāno hīyata eva |
Vai 215.010 maraṇasamaye praṇidhānaṃ kartumārabdhah—yanmayā kāśyape bhagavati samyaksambuddhe ’nuttare mahādakṣiṇīye yāvadāyurbrahmacaryaṃ cīrṇam, na kāścidguṇagaṇo ’dhigataḥ, anenāhaṃ kuśalamūlena yo ’sau bhagavatā kāśyapena samyaksambuddhenottaro nāma māṇavo varṣaśatāyuṣi prajāyāmavaśyabhāgīyakasya bhāvyatāyāṃ buddho, vyākṛtaḥ, tasya śāsane pravrajya sarvakleśaprahāṇādarhattvaṃ sākṣātkuryām |
Vai 215.014 tataḥ paścāt sārdhaṃvihāriṇa upasaṃkrāntāḥ |
te kathayanti—upādhyāya, asti kiṃcit tvayā guṇagaṇamadhigatam? sa kathayati—nāsti |
Vai 215.015 kiṃ praṇidhānaṃ kṛtam? idaṃ cedaṃ ca |
Vai 215.016 te kathayanti—vayamapyupādhyāyameva kalyāṇamitramāgamya tasyaiva bhagavato ’ntike sarvakleśaprahāṇādarhattvaṃ sākṣātkuryuḥ |
Vai 215.017 karvaṭanivāsinā janakāyena śrutaṃ glāna āryaka iti |
Vai 215.018 te ’pyupasaṃkrāntāḥ |
asti kiṃcidāryeṇa guṇagaṇamadhigatam? nāsti |
Vai 215.019 kiṃ praṇidhānaṃ kṛtam? idaṃ cedaṃ ca |
te kathayanti—vayamapyāryameva kalyāṇamitramāgamya tasyaiva bhagavato ’ntike sarvakleśaprahāṇādarhattvaṃ sākṣātkuryuḥ |
|
Vai 215.021 kiṃ manyadhve bhikṣavah? yo ’sau vaiyāvṛtyakaraḥ, eṣa evāsau saṃgharakṣito bhikṣuḥ |
Vai 215.022 yāni tāni pañca sārdhaṃvihāriśatāni, etānyeva tāni pañcabhikṣuśatāni |
yo ’sau karvaṭanivāsī janakāyaḥ, etānyeva tāni pañca vaṇikśatāni |
Vai 215.023 yadanena tara dharmavaiyāvṛtyaṃ kṛtam, tasya karmaṇo vipākenāḍhye mahādhane mahābhoge kule upapannaḥ |
Vai 215.024 yat tanmaraṇasamaye praṇidhānaṃ kṛtam, tasya karmaṇo vipākena mamāntike pravrajya sarvakleśaprahāṇādarhattvaṃ sākṣātkṛtamevaṃ ca vaineyakāryaṃ kṛtam |
|
Vai 215.027 iti hi bhikṣava ekāntakṛṣṇānāṃ karmaṇāmekāntakṛṣṇo vipākah…pūrvavat |
|
