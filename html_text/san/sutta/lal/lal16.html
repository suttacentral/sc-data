16: bimbisāropasaṃkramaṇaparivartaḥ ṣoḍaśaḥ

SC 1SC 1Vai 174evaṃ khalu bhikṣavaśchandako bodhisattvādhisthānena rājñaḥ śuddhodanasya gopāyāḥ śākyakanyāyāśca sarvasya cāntaḥpurasya sarvasya ca śākyagaṇasya śokavinodakathāmakārṣīt ||

SC 2iti hi bhikṣavo bodhisattvo lubdhakarūpāya devaputrāya kāśikāni vastrāṇi dattvā tasya sakāśātkāṣāyāni vastrāṇi gṛhītvā svayameva pravajyāṃ lokānuvartanāmupādāya sattvānukampāyai sattvaparipācanārtham ||

SC 3atha bodhisattvo yenaiva śākyā brāhmaṇyā āśramastenopasaṃkrāmat | sā bodhisattvaṃ vāsena bhaktena copanimantrayate sma | tato bodhisattvaḥ padmāyā brāhmaṇyā āśramaṃ gacchati sma | tayāpi bodhisattvo vāsena bhaktena copanimantrito ’bhūt ||

SC 4tato raivatasya brahmarṣerāśramamagamat | asāvapi bodhisattvaṃ tathaivopanimantrayate sma | tathaiva rājako ’pi datṛmadaṇḍikaputro bodhisattvamupanimantrayate sma ||

SC 5iti hi bhikṣavo bodhisattvo ’nupūrveṇa vaiśālīṃ mahānagarīmanuprāpto ’bhut ||

SC 6tena khalu punaḥ samayenārāḍaḥ kālāpo vaiśālīmupanisṛtya prativasati sma mahatā śrāvakasaṃghena sārdhaṃ tribhiḥ śiṣyaśataiḥ | sa śiṣyebhya ākiṃcanyāyatanasahavratāyai dharmaṃ deśayati sma | sa bodhisattvaṃ dūrata evāgacchantaṃ dṛṣṭvā āścaryaprāptaḥ śiṣyānāmantrayate sma—paśyata paśyata bho rūpamasyeti | te ’bruvan—evaṃ hyetatpaśyāmaḥ | enamativismayanīyam ||

SC 7tato ’haṃ bhikṣavo yenārāḍaḥ kālāpastenopasaṃkramyārāḍa kālāpametadavocat—careyamahaṃ bho ārāḍe kālāpe brahmacaryam | so ’vocat—cara bho gautama tathārūpeṇa dharmākhyāne yasmin śrāddhaḥ kulaputro ’lpakṛcchreṇājñāmārādhayati ||

SC 8tasya me bhikṣava etadabhūt—asti me chando ’sti vīryamasti smṛtirasti samādhirasti prajñā, yannvahameko ’pramatta ātāpī vyapakṛṣṭo vihareyaṃ tasyaiva dharmasya prāptaye sākṣātkriyāyai ||

SC 9atha khalvahaṃ bhikṣavo eko ’pramatta ātāpī vyapakṛṣṭo viharannalpakṛcchreṇaivaṃ taṃ dharmamadhyavagacchan sākṣādakārṣam ||

SC 10atha khalvahaṃ bhikṣavo yenārāḍaḥ kālāpastenopasaṃkramyaitadavocat—etāvadbho tvayā ārāḍa dharmo ’dhigataḥ sākṣātkṛtaḥ? so ’vocat—evametadbho gautama | tamahamavocat—mayāpi bho eṣa dharmaḥ sākṣātkṛto ’dhigataḥ | so ’vocat—tena hi bho gautama yadahaṃ dharma jānāmi, bhavānapi taṃ jānāti, yaṃ bhavān jānāti, ahamapi taṃ jānāmi | tena hyāvāmubhāvapīmaṃ śiṣyagaṇaṃ pariharāvaḥ ||

SC 11iti hi bhikṣava ārāḍaḥ kālāpaḥ paramayā pūjayā māṃ pūjayati sma | antevāsiṣu ca māṃ samānārthatayā sthāpayati sma ||SC 11iti hi bhikṣava ārāḍaḥ kālāpaḥ paramayā pūjayā māṃ pūjayati sma | antevāsiṣu ca māṃ samānārthatayā sthāpayati sma ||Vai 175

SC 12tasya me bhikṣava etadabhūt—ayaṃ khalvārāḍasya dharmo na nairyāṇiko na niryāti, tatkatarasya samyagduḥkhakṣayāya? yannvahamata uttari paryeṣamāṇaścareyam ||

SC 13atha khalvahaṃ bhikṣavo yathābhirāmaṃ vaiśālyāṃ vihṛtya magadheṣu ca prakrānto ’bhūt | so ’haṃ magadheṣu caryāṃ caran yena māgadhakānāṃ rājagṛhaṃ nagaraṃ tadanusṛto yena ca pāṇḍavaḥ parvatarājastenopasaṃkrānto ’bhūvam | tatrāhaṃ pāṇḍave parvatarājapārśve vyāhārṣamekākyadvitīyo ’sahāyo ’nekairdevakoṭinayutaśatasahasraiḥ saṃrakṣitaḥ ||

SC 14tato ’haṃ kalyameva saṃnivāsya pātracīvaramādāya tapodadvāreṇa rājagṛhaṃ mahānagaraṃ piṇḍāya prāvikṣat prāsādikenābhikrāntena pratikrāntena vyavalokitena saṃmiñjitena prasāritena prāsādikena saṃghāṭīpaṭapātracīvaradhāraṇenāvikṣiptairindriyairabahirgatena mānasena nirmitavattailapātradharavadyugamātraṃ paśyan | tatra māṃ rājagṛhakā manuṣyā dṛṣṭvā vismitā abhūvan—kiṃ svidayaṃ brahmā bhaviṣyati śakro devānāmindra āhosvidvaiśravaṇo āhosvitkiṃcidgiridaivatam ||

SC 15tatredamucyate—

SC 16atha vimaladharo hyanantatejo svayamiha pravrajiyāna bodhisattvaḥ |
śāntamanu dānta īryavanto viharati pāṇḍavaśailarājapārśve || Verse 16.1

SC 17rajani vigatu jñātva bodhisattvaḥ paramasudarśaniyaṃ nivāsayitvā |
pātra pratigṛhīya nīcamāno praviśati rājagṛhaṃ sapiṇḍapātram || Verse 16.2

SC 18kanakamiva sudhātujātarūpaṃ kavacitu lakṣaṇatriṃśatā dvibhiśca |
naragaṇa tatha nāri prekṣamāṇo na ca bhavate kvaci tṛpti darśanena || Verse 16.3

SC 19vīthi racita ratnavastradhāryai avaśiriyā janu yāti pṛṣṭhato ’sya |
ko nu ayu adṛṣṭapūrvasattvo yasya prabhāya puraṃ vibhāti sarvam || Verse 16.4

SC 20upari sthihiya nāriṇāṃ sahasrā tathariva dvāri tathaiva vātayāne |SC 20upari sthihiya nāriṇāṃ sahasrā tathariva dvāri tathaiva vātayāne |Vai 176
rathya bharita gehi śūnya kṛtvā naravaru prekṣiṣu te ananyakarmāḥ || Verse 16.5

SC 21na ca bhuyu krayavikrayaṃ karontī na ca puna sauṇḍa pibanti madyapānam |
na ca gṛhi na ca vīthiye ramante puruṣavarasya nirīkṣamāṇa rūpam || Verse 16.6

SC 22puruṣa tvaritu gacchi rājagehaṃ avaciṣu rāja sa bimbisāra tuṣṭo |
deva parama tubhya labdha lābhā svayamiha brahma pure carāti piṇḍam || Verse 16.7

SC 23keci avaci śakra devarājo apari bhaṇanti suyāma devaputraḥ |
tatha api saṃtuṣitaṃ va nirmitaśca apari bhaṇanti sunirmiteṣu devaḥ || Verse 16.8

SC 24keci puna bhaṇanti candrasūryau tathapi ca rāhu baliśca vemacitrī |
keci puna bhaṇanti vācamevaṃ ayu so pāṇḍavaśailarājavāsī || Verse 16.9

SC 25vacanamimu śruṇitva pārthivo ’sau paramaudagramanā sthito gavākṣe |
prekṣati varasattva bodhisattvaṃ jvalatu śirīya sudhātukāñcanaṃ vā || Verse 16.10

SC 26piṇḍa dadiya rāja bimbisāraḥ puruṣamavocannirīkṣa kva prayātī |
dṛṣṭva girivaraṃ sa gacchamāno avaciṣu deva gataḥ sa śailapārśvam || Verse 16.11

SC 27rajani vigatu jñātva bimbisāro mahata janaiḥ parivārito narendraḥ |
upagami pāṇḍavaśailarājamūle śiriya jvalantu tamadṛśāti śailam || Verse 16.12

SC 28SC 28Vai 177dharaṇi vrajitu yāni oruhitvā paramasugaurava prekṣi bodhisattvam |
meruriva yathā hyakampamāno nyasiya tṛṇāni niṣaṇṇa sostikena || Verse 16.13

SC 29śirasi caraṇi vandayitva rājā vividhakathāṃ samudāharitva vocat |
dadami tava upārdhu sarvarājyād rama iha kāmaguṇairahaṃ ca piṇḍam || Verse 16.14

SC 30prabhaṇati giri bodhisattva ślakṣṇaṃ dharaṇipate ciramāyu pālayasva |
ahamapi pravijahya rājyamiṣṭaṃ pravrajito nirapekṣi śāntihetoḥ || Verse 16.15

SC 31daharu taruṇayauvanairupetaḥ śubhatanuvarṇanibho ’si vegaprāptaḥ |
vipula dhana pratīccha nārisaṃghaṃ iha mama rājyi vasāhi bhuṅkṣva kāmāṃ || Verse 16.16

SC 32paramapramudito ’smi daśanātte avaciṣu sa māgadharāja bodhisattvam |
bhavahi mama sahāyu sarvarājyaṃ ahu tava dāsyi prabhūta bhuṅkṣva kāmāṃ || Verse 16.17

SC 33mā ca puna vane vasāhi śūnye ma bhuyu tṛṇeṣu vasāhi bhūmivāsam |
paramasukumāru tubhya kāyo iha mama rājyi vasāhi bhuṅkṣva kāmāṃ || Verse 16.18

SC 34prabhaṇati giri bodhisattva ślakṣṇaṃ akuṭila premaṇiyā hitānukampī |
svasti dharaṇipāla te ’stu nityaṃ na ca ahu kāmaguṇebhirarthiko ’smi || Verse 16.19

SC 35kāma viṣasamā anantadoṣā narakaprapātana pretatiryagyonau |
vidubhi vigarhita cāpyanārya kāmā jahita mayā yathā pakvakheṭapiṇḍam || Verse 16.20

SC 36SC 36Vai 178kāma drumaphalā yathā patantī yathamiva abhrabalāhakā vrajanti |
adhruva capalagāmi mārutaṃ vā vikiraṇa sarvaśubhasya vañcanīyā || Verse 16.21

SC 37kāma alabhamāna dahyayante tatha api labdha na tṛpti vindayantī |
yada puna avaśasya bhakṣayante tada mahaduḥkha janenti ghora kāmāḥ || Verse 16.22

SC 38kāma dharaṇipāla ye ca divyā tatha api mānuṣa kāma ye praṇītā |
eku naru labheta sarvakāmāṃ na ca so tṛpti labheta bhūyu eṣan || Verse 16.23

SC 39ye tu dharaṇipāla śāntadāntā ārya anāśrava dharmapūrṇasaṃjñā |
prajñaviduṣa tṛpta te sutṛptā na ca puna kāmaguṇeṣu kāci tṛptiḥ || Verse 16.24

SC 40kāma dharaṇipāla sevamānā purima na vidyati koṭi saṃskṛtasya |
lavaṇajala yathā hi nārū pītvā bhuyu tṛṣa vardhati kāma sevamāne || Verse 16.25

SC 41api ca dharaṇipāla paśya kāyaṃ adhruvamasāraku duḥkhayantrametat |
navabhi vraṇamukhaiḥ sadā sravantaṃ na mama narādhipa kāmachandarāgaḥ || Verse 16.26

SC 42ahamapi vipulāṃ vijahya kāmāṃ tathapi ca istrisahasra darśanīyāṃ |
anabhiratu bhaveṣu nirgato ’haṃ paramaśivāṃ varabodhi prāptukāmaḥ || Verse 16.27

SC 43rājā āha—

SC 44katama diśi kuto gato ’si bhikṣo kva ca tava janma kva te pitā kva mātā |SC 44katama diśi kuto gato ’si bhikṣo kva ca tava janma kva te pitā kva mātā |Vai 179
kṣatriya atha brāhmaṇo ’tha rājā parikatha bhikṣu yadī na bhārasaṃjñā || Verse 16.28

SC 45bodhisattva āha—

SC 46śratu ti dharaṇipāla śākiyānāṃ kapilapuraṃ paramaṃ suṛddhisphītam |
pitu mama śuddhodaneti nāmnā tanu ahu pravrajito guṇābhilāṣī || Verse 16.29

SC 47rājā āha—

SC 48sādhu tava sudṛṣṭadarśanaṃ te yanu tava janma vayaṃ pi tasya śiṣyāḥ |
api ca mama kṣamasva āśayenā yamapi nimantritu kāmavītarāgo || Verse 16.30

SC 49yadi tvaya anuprāptu bhoti bodhiḥ tada mama seti bhoti dharmasvāmim |
api ca mama purā sulabdha lābhā mama vijite vasasīha yatsvayaṃbho || Verse 16.31

SC 50punarapi caraṇāni vandayitvā kṛtva pradakṣiṇu gauraveṇa rājā |
svakajanaparivārito narendraḥ punarapi rājagṛhaṃ anupraviṣṭaḥ || Verse 16.32

SC 51magadhapuri praveśi lokanātho vihariya śāntamanā yathābhiprāyam |
arthu kariya devamānuṣāṇāṃ upagami tīru nirañjanā narendraḥ || Verse 16.33

SC 52|| iti śrīlalitavistare bimbisāropasaṃkramaṇaparivarto nāma ṣoḍaśamo ’dhyāyaḥ ||
