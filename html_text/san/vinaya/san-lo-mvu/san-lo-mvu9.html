• Mahāvastu

9. The Second Bhūmi

SC 1tataś ca kāśyapasthaviraḥ mahākātyāyanam abravīt* |
nirdiṣṭā prathamā bhūmi mahāsatva manoramā ||

SC 2dvitīyāṃ saṃkramantānāṃ bhūmiṃ naravarātmajā |
kiṃ cittaṃ bodhisattvānāṃ jāyate samanantaraṃ ||

SC 3ke ca adhyāśayā santi dvitīyāyāṃ jinātmaja |
bhūmiṃ tāṃ bodhisatvānāṃ yathābhūtam udīrayed iti ||

SC 4tataḥ kātyāyanaḥ sthaviraḥ kāśyapam idam abravīt* |
nirdeśaṃ bodhisatvānāṃ kīrtayiṣye manoramaṃ ||

SC 5Sen 1.85 dvitīyāṃ saṃkramantānāṃ prathamato ’nujāyate |
aratī bodhisatvānāṃ bhaveṣv iti na saṃśayaṃ ||

SC 6bodhisatvānāṃ bho jinaputra dvitīyāyāṃ bhūmau vartamānānāṃ ime adhyāśayā bhavanti | tadyathā kalyāṇādhyāśayā bhavanti snigdhādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti madhurādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti tīkṣṇādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti vipulādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti vicitrādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti gambhīrādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti aparyādinnādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti anupahatādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti asādhāraṇādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti unnatādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti akṛpaṇādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti anivartādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti akṛtrimādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti śuddhādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti dṛḍhādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti svabhāvādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti tṛptādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti pudgalādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti anantādhyāśayāś ca bhavanti ||

SC 7kathaṃ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ kalyāṇādhyāśayāḥ bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 8buddhe dharme ca saṃghe ca na kāṃkṣanti kathaṃcana |
iti adhyāśayas teṣāṃ kalyāṇa upadiśyate ||

SC 9kathaṃ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ snigdhādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 10aṅgeṣu chidyamāneṣu manas teṣāṃ na kupyate |
evam adhyāśayas teṣāṃ snigdhamṛdūpadiśyate ||

SC 11kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ madhurādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 12Sen 1.86 antaḥkuśalakarmāṇi sevanti puruṣottamāḥ |
evam adhyāśayā madhurā bhavanti dhutabuddhinām iti ||

SC 13kathaṃ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ tīkṣṇādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 14budhyanty āśayasaṃyuktā loke lokottare tathā |
evam adhyāśayā tīkṣṇā bhavanti śuddhakarmaṇām iti ||

SC 15kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā vipulādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 16sarvabhūtāna hitārthaṃ saṃcinonti śubhaṃ bahuṃ |
evam adhyāśayā vipulā bhavanti paramarṣiṇām iti ||

SC 17kathaṃ bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā vicitrādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 18vicitrāṇi manojñāṇi denti dānāny amatsarāḥ |
evam adhyāśayā vicitrā bhavanty uttamadarṣinām iti ||

SC 19kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā aparyādinnādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 20aparyādinnacittās te prativedhaparākramāḥ |
evam adhyāśayas teṣām aparyādinna ucyate ||

SC 21kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā anupahatādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 22na te śakyanti saṃhartuṃ duṣṭacittena kenacit* |
evam adhyāśayas teṣāṃ na jātu upahanyate ||

SC 23Sen 1.87 kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā asādhāraṇādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 24yaṃ nānyaḥ praṇidhiḥ kaścit* evaṃ satvasyopajāyate |
sarvasatvasukhārthāya tad asādhāraṇaṃ vidur iti ||

SC 25kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā unnatādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 26aparatīrthikamataṃ śrutvāvajñā pratiṣṭhate |
unnatādhyāśayās tena narasiṃhā bhavanti te ti ||

SC 27kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā akṛpaṇādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 28na kāmaguṇabhogārthaṃ saṃcinvanti śubhaṃ vidūḥ |
tataś cākṛpaṇās teṣāṃ bhavanty adhyāśayā sadeti ||

SC 29kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā anivartiyādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 30kāmaiḥ te nāvakīryante buddhatve kṛtaniścayāḥ |
tenānivartiyās teṣām adhyāśayā iti smṛtāḥ ||

SC 31kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā akṛtrimādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 32vaśi pratyekabuddhānāṃ na spṛhenti kathaṃcana |
evaṃ cākṛtrimo bhavati teṣām adhyāśayaḥ sadeti ||

SC 33kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ śuddhādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 34Sen 1.88 lābhasatkāram utsṛjya paramārthābhikāṃkṣiṇo |
śuddha adhyāśayas teṣām ity evam upadiśyate ||

SC 35kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā dṛḍhādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 36na saṃharanti vīriyaṃ dharme lokair abhidrutā |
evam adhyāśayās teṣāṃ dṛḍhā santi maharṣiṇām iti ||

SC 37kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ svabhāvādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 38mūrchitās te na bhuṃjanti parānnāny avasrutā |
svabhāvādhyāśayas teṣām evam ārya praśaṃsyate ti ||

SC 39kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ tṛptādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 40nātra prasyandanti kāmeṣu niṣkramyābhiratāḥ sadā |
evam adhyāśayo tṛpto bodhisatve praśaṃsyate ti ||

SC 41kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvāḥ pudgalādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 42svayaṃbhūsarvadarśitvam abhikāṅkṣanti paṇḍitāḥ |
pudgalādhyāśayā bhavanti caivam apratimā dhruvāḥ ||

SC 43Sen 1.89 kathaṃ ca bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā anantādhyāśayā bhavanti || ucyate ||

SC 44na prārthayanti mahābhogān adānaguṇasaṃpadā |
anantādhyāśayāś caivaṃ bhavanti puruṣottamā iti ||

SC 45sarvehi etehi viṃśadbhiḥ sarvadharmaviśāradā |
samanvitā satpuruṣā śubhair adhyāśayair varā iti ||

SC 46imehi khalu bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā viṃśadbhir adhyāśayaiḥ samanvāgatā bhavantīti ||

SC 47evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākāśyapa āyuṣmantaṃ mahākātyāyanam uvāca || katihi bho jinaputra ākārehi bodhisatvā dvitīyāyāṃ bhūmau vartamānās tṛtīyāyāṃ bhūmau vivartanti || evam ukte āyuṣmān mahākātyāyano āyuṣmantaṃ mahākāśyapam uvāca || aṣṭāviṃśadbhiḥ bho dhutadharmadhara kāraṇehi bodhisatvā dvitīyāyāṃ bhūmau vartamānāḥ tṛtīyāyāṃ bhūmau vivartante || katamair aṣṭāviṃśadbhir ākāraiḥ || tadyathā lābhagurukāś ca bhavanti | satkāragurukāś ca bhavanti | kīrtiślokaparāś ca bhavanti | śaṭhāś ca bhavanti | viṣameṇa ca vṛddhiṃ kalpayanti | gurukopanabhāṣaṇaparāś ca bhavanti | triṣu rataneṣu na ca citrikārabahulā vitaranti | dakṣiṇīyeṣu bodhisatvacaritaṃ ca na paryeṣanti | yataś ca bodhisatvacaritabhūmiṃ prāpnuvanti tāṃ na pūjayanti | atirekapūjāye prāpyaṃ ca bhāraṃ na upādiyanti | aprāpyaṃ ca bhāraṃ upādiyitvā vitaranti | ākīrṇavihāreṇa ca nārtīyanti | mālyavastrālaṃkārābhāraṇānulepanadharāś ca bhavanti | alpaguṇaparituṣṭāś Sen 1.90 ca bhavanti | abhīkṣṇaṃ lokaramaṇīyābhiratāś ca bhavanti | na ca sarvadhātū anityā saṃkalpayanti | svena ca varṇarūpeṇa param abhimanyanti | na ca viparītadarśanatyāgaṃ karonti | na ca yathoddiṣṭaṃ padavyaṃjanaṃ paripūrṇaṃ karonti | deśanāmatsariṇaś ca bhavanti | apātradarśinaś ca bhavanti | pātre ca na pratipādayanti | kaṭhinasantānāś ca bhavanti | asamīkṣakāriṇaś ca bhavanti || ye keci bho dhutadharmadhara bodhisatvā dvitīyāyāṃ bhūmau vartamānā tṛtīyāyāṃ bhūmau vivartanti sarve te imehi-r-aṣṭāviṃśadbhir ākārair vivartanti ||

SC 48tatra idam iti ucyate ||

SC 49ity eṣā dvitīyā bhūmi bodhisatvānam ucyate |
nānākuśalakośānāṃ lokārthasukhacāriṇāṃ ||

SC 50ye hi doṣehi saṃyuktā vivartanti tathāvidhā |
ye caivaṃ parivartantā na vivartanti paṇḍitāḥ ||

SC 51durārohāṃ dhuradhīrāḥ pratipadyanti śūratāṃ |
tāṃ ca lokānukampārthaṃ bahuduḥkhā caranti te ||

SC 52te te devamanuṣyāṇāṃ pūjyāḥ sarve tathāgatāḥ |
tathā hi vividhaṃ duḥkhaṃ upenti jñānapūrvakaṃ ||

SC 53nānādhātum imaṃ lokam anuvartanti paṇḍitāḥ |
tena teṣāṃ gatā kīrti loke samarumānuṣe iti ||

iti śrīmahāvastu-avadāne dvitīyabhūmiḥ samāptā ||
