10.52. Sāriputta

There the Venerable Sāriputta addressed the bhikkhus: “Friends, bhikkhus!”

“Friend!” those bhikkhus replied. The Venerable Sāriputta said this:

Identical with 10:51, but spoken by Sāriputta. [95–96]
