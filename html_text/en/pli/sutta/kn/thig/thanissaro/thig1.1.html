1.1. An Anonymous Bhikkhuni

Sleep, little theri, sleep comfortably,
wrapped in the robe that you've made,
for your passion is stilled —
like a pot of pickled greens
boiled dry.

Translated from the Pali by: Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
