5.12. Canda: The Beggar

SC 1SC Verse 122PTS Verse 122Ms 19Th2_175SC 1SC Verse 122PTS Verse 122Ms 19Th2_175Msdiv 122Before, I had fallen on evil times:
no husband, no children,
no relatives, friends,
no way to obtain clothing & food.

SC 2SC Verse 123PTS Verse 123Ms 19Th2_176SC 2SC Verse 123PTS Verse 123Ms 19Th2_176Msdiv 123So, taking a staff & bowl in hand,
begging for alms from house to house,
feverish from the cold & heat,
I wandered for seven full years.

SC 3SC Verse 124PTS Verse 124Ms 19Th2_177SC 3SC Verse 124PTS Verse 124Ms 19Th2_177Msdiv 124Then seeing a nun
obtaining food & drink,
I approached her & said:
"Let me go forth into homelessness."

SC 4SC Verse 125PTS Verse 125Ms 19Th2_178SC 4SC Verse 125PTS Verse 125Ms 19Th2_178Msdiv 125She, Patacara, from sympathy,
let me go forth;
then, exhorting me,
urged me on to the highest goal.

SC 5SC Verse 126PTS Verse 126Ms 19Th2_179SC 5SC Verse 126PTS Verse 126Ms 19Th2_179Msdiv 126Hearing her words,
I did her bidding.
Her exhortation was not in vain.
I'm a three-knowledge woman,
fermentation-free.
