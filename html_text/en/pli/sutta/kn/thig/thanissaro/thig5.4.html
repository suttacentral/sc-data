5.4. Nanda's Vision

SC 1SC Verse 82PTS Verse 82Ms 19Th2_127SC 1SC Verse 82PTS Verse 82Ms 19Th2_127Msdiv 82"Sick, putrid, unclean:
look, Nanda, at this physical heap.
Through contemplation of the foul,
develop your mind,
make it one, well-centered.

SC 2SC Verse 83PTS Verse 83Ms 19Th2_128SC 2SC Verse 83PTS Verse 83Ms 19Th2_128Msdiv 83As this [your body], so that.
As that, so this.
It gives off a foul stench,
the delight of fools."

SC 3SC Verse 84PTS Verse 84Ms 19Th2_129SC 3SC Verse 84PTS Verse 84Ms 19Th2_129Msdiv 84Considering it thus,
untiring, both day & night,
I, with my own discernment
dissecting it,
saw.

SC 4SC Verse 85PTS Verse 85Ms 19Th2_130SC 4SC Verse 85PTS Verse 85Ms 19Th2_130Msdiv 85And as I, heedful,
examined it aptly,
this body — as it actually is —
was seen inside & out.

SC 5SC Verse 86PTS Verse 86Ms 19Th2_131SC 5SC Verse 86PTS Verse 86Ms 19Th2_131Msdiv 86Then was I disenchanted with the body
& dispassionate within:
Heedful, detached,
calmed was I.
Unbound.
