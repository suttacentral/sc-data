The Young Man Kappa’s Questions

SC 1SC Verse 1099SVV 5.117PTS Verse 1092SC 1SC Verse 1099SVV 5.117PTS Verse 1092SVSV 5.11.1“For those standing in the middle of a lake,” said venerable Kappa,
“when a fearful flood has arisen,
for those overcome by old age and death, speak about an island, dear Sir,
you must explain an island to me, so there will be no more after this.”

SC 2SC Verse 1100SVV 5.118PTS Verse 1093SC 2SC Verse 1100SVV 5.118PTS Verse 1093SVSV 5.11.2“For those standing in the middle of a lake, Kappa,” said the Gracious One,
“when a fearful flood has arisen,
for those overcome by old age and death, I speak about an island, Kappa:

SC 3SC Verse 1101SVV 5.119PTS Verse 1094SC 3SC Verse 1101SVV 5.119PTS Verse 1094SVSV 5.11.3“Having nothing, no attachment, this is the island with nothing beyond,
this is called Nibbāna, I say, the end of old age and death.

SC 4SC Verse 1102SVV 5.120PTS Verse 1095SC 4SC Verse 1102SVV 5.120PTS Verse 1095SVSV 5.11.4“Knowing this, those who are mindful, who are emancipated in this very life,
come not under Māra’s control, they are not servants to Māra.”

The Young Man Kappa’s Questions are Finished
