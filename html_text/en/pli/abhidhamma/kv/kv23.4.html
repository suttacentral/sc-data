23.4 Of Counterfeit States of Consciousness

Controverted Point: That there is that which is not (a) lust, (b) hate, (c) dullness, (d) the corruptions, but which counterfeits each of them.

Theravādin: PTS cs 23.4.1(a) Do you imply that there is that which is not contact, not feeling, not perceiving, not volition, not cognition, not faith, not energy, not mindfulness, not concentration, not understanding, but which simulates each of these?

PTS cs 23.4.2Similarly for (b), (c), (d).
