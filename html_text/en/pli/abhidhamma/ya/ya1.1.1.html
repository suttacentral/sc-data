• 1. Pairs on Roots (Mūla Yamaka)

1.1.1. Enumeration Chapter on Roots (Mūlavāra Uddessa)

1. Set of Four Methods on Faultless Section (Kusala-Pada Naya-Catukka)

  1. PTS 1.1 All faultless states (are present).
  2. Are they all faultless roots?
  3. These faultless roots (are present).
  4. Are they all faultless states?
  1. PTS 1.2All faultless states.
  2. Have they all the same roots as the faultless roots?
  3. These have the same roots as the faultless roots.
  4. Are they all faultless states?
  1. PTS 1.3All these states have the same roots as the faultless roots.
  2. Have they all mutual roots with the faultless roots?
  3. These have mutual roots with the faultless roots.
  4. Are they all faultless states? (1)

  1. PTS 1.7All faultless states.

  1. PTS 1.8All faultless states.

  3. These have the same roots as the faultless roots.

  1. PTS 1.9All these states have the same roots as the faultless roots.

  1. PTS 1.11All faultless states.
