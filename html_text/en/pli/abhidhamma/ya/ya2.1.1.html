• 2. Pairs on Aggregates (Khandha Yamaka)

2.1.1. Summary Chapter on Terms (Paṇṇattivāra Uddesa)

PTS 2.1(There are) five aggregates: matter aggregate, feeling aggregate, perception aggregate, mental formations aggregate and consciousness aggregate.
