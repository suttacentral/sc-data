• 9. Pairs on Dhammas (Dhamma Yamaka)

9.3. Chapter on Developing (Bhāvanā Vāra)

This PTS 9.229 person develops faultless dhamma. Does that person abandon un-faultless dhamma? Yes.

This person abandons un-faultless dhamma. Does that person develop faultless dhamma? Yes.

This person does not develop faultless dhamma. Does that person not abandon un-faultless dhamma? Yes.

This person does not abandon un-faultless dhamma. Does that person not develop faultless dhamma? Yes. … pe …

End of Chapter on Developing.

End of Pairs on Dhammas.
