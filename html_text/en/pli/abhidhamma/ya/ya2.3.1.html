2.3.1. Chapter on the Present (Paccupannavāra)

PTS 2.206This person comprehends matter aggregate. Does that person comprehend feeling aggregate? Yes.

This person comprehends feeling aggregate. Does that person comprehend matter aggregate? Yes.

This person does not comprehend matter aggregate. Does that person not comprehend feeling aggregate? Yes.
