2.3.3. Chapter on the Future (Anagatavāra)

PTS 2.208This person will comprehend matter aggregate.

Will that person comprehend feeling aggregate? Yes.

This person will comprehend feeling aggregate.

Will that person comprehend matter aggregate?

This person will not comprehend matter aggregate.

Will that person not comprehend feeling aggregate? Yes.

This person will not comprehend feeling aggregate.

Will that person not comprehend matter aggregate? Yes.
