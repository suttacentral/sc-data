3.3.3. Chapter on the Future (Anagatavāra)

PTS 3.251This person will comprehend eye base. Will that person comprehend ear base? Yes.

This person will comprehend ear base. Will that person comprehend eye base? Yes.

This person will not comprehend eye base. Will that person not comprehend ear base? Yes.

This person will not comprehend ear base. Will that person not comprehend eye base? Yes.
