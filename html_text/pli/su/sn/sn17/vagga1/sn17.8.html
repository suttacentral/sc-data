Saṃyutta Nikāya 17

1. Paṭhamavagga

8. Siṅgālasutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … adhigamāya. Assuttha no tumhe, bhikkhave, rattiyā paccūsasamayaṃ jarasiṅgālassa vassamānassā”ti? “Evaṃ, bhante”. “Eso kho, bhikkhave, jarasiṅgālo ukkaṇḍakena nāma rogajātena phuṭṭho neva bilagato ramati, na rukkhamūlagato ramati, na ajjhokāsagato ramati; yena yena gacchati, yattha yattha tiṭṭhati, yattha yattha nisīdati, yattha yattha nipajjati; tattha tattha anayabyasanaṃ āpajjati. Evameva kho, bhikkhave, idhekacco bhikkhu lābhasakkā­ra­silokena abhibhūto ­pariyā­diṇṇa­citto neva suññāgāragato ramati, na rukkhamūlagato ramati, na ajjhokāsagato ramati; yena yena gacchati, yattha yattha tiṭṭhati, yattha yattha nisīdati, yattha yattha nipajjati; tattha tattha anayabyasanaṃ āpajjati. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
