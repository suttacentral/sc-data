Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

1.24. Sugandhattheragāthā

“Anuvassiko pabbajito,
passa dhammasudhammataṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Sugandho thero ….
