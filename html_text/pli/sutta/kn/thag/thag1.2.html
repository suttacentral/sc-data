Ekakanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

1.2. Mahākoṭṭhikattheragāthā

“Upasanto uparato,
mantabhāṇī anuddhato;
Dhunāti pāpake dhamme,
dumapattaṃva māluto”ti.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā mahākoṭṭhiko thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
