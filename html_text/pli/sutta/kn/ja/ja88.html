88. Sārambhajātaka

“Kalyāṇimeva muñceyya,
na hi muñceyya pāpikaṃ;
Mokkho kalyāṇiyā sādhu,
mutvā tappati pāpikan”ti.

Sārambhajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
