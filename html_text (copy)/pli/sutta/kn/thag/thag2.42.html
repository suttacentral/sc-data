Pañcamavagga

2.42. Dhammapālattheragāthā

“Yo have daharo bhikkhu,
yuñjati buddhasāsane;
Jāgaro sa hi suttesu,
amoghaṃ tassa jīvitaṃ.

Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,
pasādaṃ dhammadassanaṃ;
Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,
saraṃ buddhāna sāsanan”ti.

… Dhammapālo thero ….
